Cheetah Habitat

  • Cheetahs live in most parts of Southern and Eastern Africa.
    • The rare Asiatic Cheetah lives in a few places in Iran as well.
  • They prefer the Grasslands as their home.
    • Their fur coats can help them blend in with the grass and hunt their prey.
    • Their coats also depend on their current habitat.
    • The Grasslands are known as the Savannas in Africa.
    • The Grasslands contain flat and open areas.
    • Rainfall is little there as well.
    • Elephants, zebras, lions rhinos, hippos, and other animals live there too.
